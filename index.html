IIIT Times : Economic Data Visualisation
  Created by: Aman,Yash,Divij
  This Data visualization aims to study the economic trends between the years 2008 to 2021.
  These trends are made to aid policy makers, economists and financial advisors in making sound
  economic decisions and analysis.

Global Visualization 

        We are comparing economic parameters and comparing them over the years 2008 to 2021. A
        lighter shade corresponds to a lower value and a darker shade corresponds to a larger value.
        Users can select the parameter of choice and view the global trends in the parameter during the
        time 2008-2021

Country Visualization - I

  In this visualisation we are analyzing the economic parameters of a particular country. This
  visualisation lets the users compare the GDP,GDP PPP and GDP per capita income which will
  help the user get and compare absolute values for the visualisation.

Country Visualization - II

    In this visualisation we are analyzing the economic parameters of a particular country. This
    visualisation lets the users compare the Debt (as % of GDP), Inflation rate, Unemployment rate
    to find the relevant trends in the data.

Continents Visualization 

  In this visualisation we are comparing the continents to each other. This stacked bar chart also
  allows the user to compare the proportional importance of the most economically influential
  countries in the continent's economic parameters.
